About

CCBB Was created as a second year university group project, as part of our 3D Game Development module. Winning best group at the 2023 End of year show.
Conveyer Craze Burger Builder is a silly game about stacking sandwiches on a moving conveyer belt to customers orders, Heavily inspired by the Papa's series of games, you must keep customers happy, obeying their orders correctly on a day to day basis.
As the days pass you unlock new ingredients and customers orders grow in complexity and numbers creating a tense progressive experience, score high by collecting cash along the way. and any bonus items that appear throughout playtime.

My role

Although the game was simplistic in nature it had many features that needed to be implemented and implemented well to compliment the overall gameplay experience, a focus on polish, playability and accessibility were key throughout development.
My roles mainly covered the Drag&Drop system of ingredients, stacking functionality for sandwiches as well as the all of the physics used for the game logic, detection of ingredients and score accumulation, in addition I was also responsible for the ticket/order generation and Ticket UI.
